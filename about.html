Introduction to the practice Who am I and what will I cover?

Hello, my name is Brigham Mortensen, and I am currently a pre-law, business student at Brigham Young University. With a passion for justice and a keen interest in the intricacies of law, I aspire to become a lawyer specializing in immigration, bankruptcy, tax, and criminal defense. My journey towards this goal is fueled by a desire to make a meaningful difference in people's lives by navigating complex legal systems and advocating for those in need. Through my academic pursuits and extracurricular activities, I am dedicated to honing my analytical skills, deepening my understanding of the law, and preparing myself to serve my future clients with integrity, empathy, and dedication.
